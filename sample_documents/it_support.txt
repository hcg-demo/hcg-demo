# IT Support Guide

## Password Reset
To reset your password:
1. Go to https://password.company.com
2. Click "Forgot Password"
3. Enter your employee ID
4. Follow the email instructions
5. Your new password must be at least 12 characters with uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and symbols

## VPN Access
1. Download Cisco AnyConnect from IT Portal
2. Install the application
3. Connect to vpn.company.com
4. Use your AD credentials
5. Enable 2FA with your mobile device

## Laptop Issues
For hardware issues:
- Email: itsupport@company.com
- Call: +65 6123 4567
- ServiceNow: Create incident ticket
- Response time: 4 hours for critical issues

## Software Installation
All software requests must go through ServiceNow approval process.
